Event Guidelines

  • Registration Time: Be there at the Registration Desk by 9:30 am sharp.
  • ID Card Must: Don't forget your college ID card. No ID card means no entry to the campus.
  • Participation Limits:
    • Each student can join a maximum of 2 events.
    • Pick one Technical and one Non-Technical event.
    • Alternatively, you can go for two Technical or two Non-Technical events.
  • Event Variety: Mix it up! Choose from a variety of technical and non-technical events to showcase your skills.
  • Lunch and Snacks: Don't worry about hunger! Lunch and snacks will be provided to keep you energized throughout the day.
  • Fair Play: Play fair! Follow the rules and spirit of the event for an enjoyable experience.
  • Team Size: Pay attention to the team size specified for each event. Some may be solo, while others require a team.
  • Preparedness: Come prepared for the event requirements. Whether it's a laptop, materials, or your enthusiasm, make sure you're ready.
  • Respect Guidelines: Follow event-specific guidelines. They're there to ensure a smooth and fair competition.
  • Enjoy the Experience: Most importantly, have fun! Enjoy the event, meet new people, and make the most of this exciting opportunity.
