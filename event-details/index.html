Event Details

Technical Events

Paper Presentation - Team of 3 Welcome!

Dive into the realm of ideas and innovation! Paper Presentation is your canvas to express thoughts, share discoveries, and captivate the audience with your brilliance.

Team Size: #DynamicTrio 🤝 Gather your dynamic trio! A maximum of 3 members is all you need for this thrilling event.

Paper Perfection: #BringYourAGame 🚀 Bring your A-game! Ensure you have the updated final paper on soft copy. Let's transform your presentation into a masterpiece!


Project Expo - Dynamic Duos Unite!

Project Expo is where innovation meets craftsmanship! Showcase your creativity by presenting projects featuring captivating hardware equipment. It's your time to shine with hardware brilliance!

Team Size: #DynamicDuo 🤝 Grab your partner in innovation! A duo is perfect for this adventure.

Project Requirement: #HardwareFocus 🔧 Let's keep it hardware-centric! Your project should exclusively feature hardware equipment.


Web Design - Solo Creative Voyage!

Unleash your creativity in the digital realm! Web Design is your canvas to craft visually stunning websites, showcasing your artistic flair and technical prowess. Get ready to bring your digital dreams to life!

Solo Adventure: #Solo 🚀 Grab your partner in innovation – yourself! This solo adventure is the perfect opportunity to showcase your individual brilliance.

On-the-Spot Development: #CreateOnTheSpot 🎨 No prepping needed! Participants should develop their design right here, on the spot. Let the creativity flow in real-time!

Topics Unveiled on the Spot: #UnveilTheTopics 📜 Excitement awaits! The topics about the design will be declared on the spot, adding an element of surprise to your creative process.

Non-Technical Events

Just a Minute - Solo Expressive Journey!

Immerse yourself in the art of spontaneous expression! 'Just a Minute' is your platform to share thoughts, ideas, and stories in 60 seconds, making every moment count in this dynamic and fast-paced event.

Solo Spotlight: #Solo 🚀 Solo participants, this is your stage! Embrace the spotlight and let your words captivate the audience.

Immediate Responses #QuickThinker 💬 Participants, here's the challenge – answer the question immediately, without discussions with others. It's all about your unique perspective and quick thinking!

Winner Declaration #MarksMatter 🏆 The winner will be declared based on marks, so give it your all and make those minutes count!


Mobile Photography - Solo Visual Adventure!

Dive into the world of creativity through your lens! Mobile Photography is your chance to turn ordinary moments into extraordinary memories. Capture the essence of our campus through your unique perspective.

Solo Adventure: #SoloExplorer 🚀 This is your solo adventure! Unleash your creativity and let your photographic skills shine.

Capture Within Campus: #CampusChronicles 📸 Your canvas is within the campus! Explore the beauty and stories that unfold in our surroundings. Every click is a piece of the campus narrative.

Pre-Upload Your Masterpieces: #PreUploadMagic 🌟 Here's the twist – participants, get ready to showcase your talent! Upload your captivating photos before the specified time and let the anticipation build.


Poster Designing - Solo Creative Canvas!

Immerse yourself in the world of design and storytelling! Poster Designing is your chance to transform ideas into captivating visuals. Let your imagination run wild as you create a visual narrative that speaks volumes.

Solo Spotlight: #SoloCreative 🎨 Solo participants, this stage is all yours! Embrace the canvas and let your artistic expressions take center stage.

Design Your Idea On the Spot #OnTheSpotCreation 🖌️ No need to prepare in advance! Participants, design your idea right here, on the spot. Let the creative sparks fly!

Topics Unveiled on the Spot: #SpotlightTopics 🌟 Excitement awaits! The topics for your poster design will be revealed on the spot, adding an element of surprise and spontaneity to your creative process.

Event Guidelines

  • Registration Time: Be there at the Registration Desk by 9:30 am sharp.
  • ID Card Must: Don't forget your college ID card. No ID card means no entry to the campus.
  • Participation Limits:
    • Each student can join a maximum of 2 events.
    • Pick one Technical and one Non-Technical event.
    • Alternatively, you can go for two Technical or two Non-Technical events.
  • Event Variety: Mix it up! Choose from a variety of technical and non-technical events to showcase your skills.
  • Lunch and Snacks: Don't worry about hunger! Lunch and snacks will be provided to keep you energized throughout the day.
  • Fair Play: Play fair! Follow the rules and spirit of the event for an enjoyable experience.
  • Team Size: Pay attention to the team size specified for each event. Some may be solo, while others require a team.
  • Preparedness: Come prepared for the event requirements. Whether it's a laptop, materials, or your enthusiasm, make sure you're ready.
  • Respect Guidelines: Follow event-specific guidelines. They're there to ensure a smooth and fair competition.
  • Enjoy the Experience: Most importantly, have fun! Enjoy the event, meet new people, and make the most of this exciting opportunity.
